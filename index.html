About Mathieu

With over 15 years of experience behind the lens, Mathieu Frigon has developed a distinctive visual language that blends technical precision with artistic intuition. His work has been featured in international exhibitions and publications, earning recognition for its depth and emotional resonance.

Based between Montreal and Paris, Mathieu approaches each project with a unique perspective, whether working on commercial assignments or personal fine art series.
